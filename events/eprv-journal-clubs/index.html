EPRV RCN Journal Clubs

Starting in March 2026, the RCN will run two monthly EPRV journal clubs in an effort to accomodate our members' wide range of time-zones. These clubs will be independent events, and so the papers discussed at each club will likely be different.

The clubs will take place on:

  • The First Thursday of each month at 4p Uk / 11a Eastern US / 8a Pacific US
  • The Third Tuesday of each month at 5p Eastern US / 2p Pacific US / 8a Eastern Australia

Both series have already been added to the RCN Google Calendar, and the Zoom links and individual .ics meeting invites were also distributed via the RCN mailing list in February 2026.

We'll be using benty-fields to organize both journal clubs. You can find our specific clubs by searching for 'EPRV' on the Join Journal Club page. We've also set up a new #eprv-journal-clubs channel in the RCN slack for conversations about the journal clubs and/or questions about using benty-fields.

Key Journal Club Logistics

  • Each journal club will be one hour long, and we will aim to have 2-3 people lead paper discussions in that time
  • Paper discussions will be low-stakes: no slides are required and we encourage the discussion leaders to just scroll through the paper on screen
  • We welcome votes for both new papers and older papers (using the Benty-Search option) as long as you think they're relevant to the EPRV community
  • Please use the “volunteer” button on the specific-journal club pages (see last page of the attached PDF for example) to offer to lead a paper discussion
  • We'll aim to decide on the top three papers for each club 24-48 hours in advance and confirm with the volunteers that they are still able to present.
  • With the discussion leaders' permission, we will record the paper discussions and post them to the RCN's YouTube channel in a new Journal Club playlist so that more RCN members can catch these helpful literature recaps
