Upcoming EPRV Related Events

EPRV Journal Clubs

Starting in March 2026, the RCN will run two monthly EPRV journal clubs in an effort to accomodate our members' wide range of time-zones. These clubs will be independent events, and so the papers discussed at each club will likely be different.
The clubs will take place on:

  • The First Thursday of each month at 4p Uk / 11a Eastern US / 8a Pacific US
  • The Third Tuesday of each month at 5p Eastern US / 2p Pacific US / 8a Eastern Australia

    • For more information on journal club format and logistics, please click on the link above.

EPRV RCN Colloquia

The next EPRV RCN Colloquium will take place on April 23rd, 2026 at 8am Pacific US time and feature Dr. Jessi Cisewski-Kehe [University of Wisconsin-Madison]!

For connection info, please refer to the EPRV RCN Google Calendar and/or email updates, available to RCN members.
