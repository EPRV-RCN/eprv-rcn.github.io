Upcoming EPRV Related Events

EPRV RCN Colloquia

The next EPRV RCN Colloquium will take place on June 25th, 2026 at 8am Pacific US time and feature Dr. Sowmya Krishnamurthy [University of Graz] who will speak about tracing the spectral signatures of stellar granulation and magnetic activity using 3D MHD simulations!

For connection info, please refer to the EPRV RCN Google Calendar and/or email updates, available to RCN members.

EPRV x Solar Physics On-line Workshop

The Extreme Precision Radial Velocity Research Coordination Network (EPRV RCN) will host an on-line workshop focused on the synergies between EPRV and solar physics on Wednesday, June 10th & Thursday, 11th from 3:00 - 5:30p UTC / 8:00 - 10:30a Pacific US time each day. The goal of this workshop is to explore potential synergies amongst data/facilities/analysis methods between EPRV solar facilities and the solar physics community. Ideally this will raise awareness of the resources developed by each community that may be of assistance to the other and potentially spark new collaboration ideas!

Click the heading above for more info on the workshop, the full agenda, and connection information!
