EPRV Resources

Pages containing useful resources for all things EPRV

  • Introduction to EPRV: Resources for those new to the field or looking to brush up with some EPRV overviews
  • EPRV Presentations: Links to conference presentations and colloquia related to EPRV
  • EPRV Tutorials: Tutorials for accessing and working with EPRV data products
  • EPRV Visual Resources: Plots, figures, animations, etc contributed by the EPRV community
  • EPRV Observing Resources: Information on EPRV instruments and how to apply for observing time
  • EPRV ADS Libraries: Compilations of EPRV-related journal articles and conference proceedings
